Reference Notes

            


              

                Time inputs —
                Enter hours and minutes without a colon (e.g. 745 or 0745 for 7:45). Colon format may not be recognized on all mobile keyboards.
              


              

                Rounding —
                All time values are displayed as hh:mm, but calculations use full precision internally. Minor rounding differences (e.g. a result showing 10:02 instead of 10:00) are expected and reflect how fractional minutes accumulate.
              


              

                ALPP days —
                Treated as on-call days for pay purposes. For example, if the calculation yields 17 on-call days and you have 2 ALPP days, PBS fills the remaining 15. Note that ALPP days do not count as work when determining block lengths for reserve line construction — RES RES ALPP ALPP is a two-day work block, not four.
              


              

                Carry-in pairings —
                Count as on-call days with flying already assigned. If you have 17 on-call days and 3 carry-in days, PBS assigns 14 additional days. Carry-in credit only affects pay if it exceeds the guarantee.
              


              

                Drop / IVD —
                After PBS, on-call days are valued at the reserve pro-rata share (guarantee ÷ on-call days, per PWA 14.A.11). ML, PD, APD, or sick days that overlap an on-call day each deduct that value from the guarantee. For IVD, drop the day and replace it with a vacation-rate day.
              


              

                Standards meetings —
                Enter each meeting day (and any associated travel day) as an "Other" line item at 5:00 per day.
              


              

                Parental leave (pre-posted only) —
                The pay calculations here apply only to pre-posted parental leave.
